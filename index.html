Aeration & Seeding in Indiana

Here at Pro-Turf Indiana, we offer quality aeration and seeding services that revitalize and nourish your lawn. Aeration reduces soil compaction by removing small plugs of soil, which improves the absorption of air, water, and nutrients, promoting stronger root growth. Overseeding involves spreading grass seed over your existing lawn to thicken it and enhance its appearance.

How Our Core Aeration Service Works

Our team uses professional-grade core aerators to reduce soil compaction that naturally occurs over time. A technician moves the machine across your lawn, removing small plugs or "cores" of soil. These plugs (about 1-2 inches long) are left on the lawn to break down naturally, returning valuable nutrients to the soil. The holes created allow your lawn's root system to breathe and grow deeper.

The Importance of Overseeding

The holes created by aeration provide the perfect environment for grass seeds to make direct contact with the soil, dramatically improving germination rates. By spreading premium grass seed over the aerated lawn, overseeding helps fill in bare spots, thicken the turf, and enhance overall lawn density. This combination promotes a healthier, more resilient lawn that can better withstand environmental stress and resist weeds.
